Welcome to Hacker News Redux

With Hacker News Redux you can enjoy reading Hacker News articles and comments even when you are offline.

Its preload mode let’s you download the latest news in advance, making it the ideal reader when your Internet connection becomes unreliable like on subways or in-flight.

• Preload mode to download the latest articles and comments for offline reading

• Readability support for a distraction-free reading experience

• Article preview in the news feed

• Completely native iOS user-interface for easy-of-use

• Bookmarking of articles and comments

• Sharing via Twitter, Facebook or E-Mail
