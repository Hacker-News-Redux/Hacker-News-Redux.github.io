Welcome to Hacker News Redux!

With Hacker News Redux you can enjoy reading articles and comments even when you are offline.

Seamlessly continue browsing and reading Hacker News with poor or no Internet connectivtity like on subways or in-flight.

  • Downloads the latest news in advance for offline reading

  • Built-in Readability support for distraction-free reading experience

  • Article preview in the news feed

  • Bookmarking of articles and comments

  • Full support for iPhone and iPad with completely native iOS user-interface

  • Sharing via Twitter, Facebook or E-Mail

Questions? Drop me an e-mail

Hacker News Redux would not have been possible without these open-source projects: Cocoanetics DTCoreText, Mozilla Readability, Alamofire
